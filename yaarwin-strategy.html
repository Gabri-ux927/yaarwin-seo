Simple Yaarwin Strategy for Beginners

Forget complex tactics. Here's the 3-step strategy that works from day one.

Step 1: Start Small

Don't try everything at once. Pick ONE prediction type and master it first.

Step 2: Follow the Pattern, Not Your Gut

The most common mistake is guessing randomly. Yaarwin gives you patterns — follow them for 10 tries before changing anything.

Step 3: Track Everything

Write down each prediction and the outcome. After 20 predictions, you'll see what works for YOU.

Pro Tip for Beginners

Don't change strategy after one loss. Give any strategy at least 10 attempts before judging it.
