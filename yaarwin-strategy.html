Smart strategies to improve your colour prediction game. No guaranteed wins — just proven approaches.

🎯 Strategy 1: Bankroll Management

Never bet more than 5% of your total balance on a single round.
Example: If you have ₹1000, max bet = ₹50 per round.
This ensures you can survive losing streaks.

📊 Strategy 2: Pattern Tracking

Watch 10-15 rounds before placing real bets. Note which colors appear most frequently.
Many players use the "3-round rule" — if Red hasn't appeared in 3 rounds, bet on Red.

💰 Strategy 3: The 70% Rule

Withdraw 70% of your winnings daily. Leave 30% to continue playing.
This locks in profits and prevents giving everything back.
