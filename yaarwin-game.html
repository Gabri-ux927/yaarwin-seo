Yaarwin Game Rules

Welcome to Yaarwin. Please read the official game rules before participating.

🎨 Available Options

  • Colors: Red, Green, Violet
  • Sizes: Big, Small
  • Numbers: 1 to 9

📋 Important Rules

  • Only one prediction per period
  • Cannot bet on both Big and Small in same period
  • Cannot bet on all possible outcomes
  • Cannot combine Big/Small with colors

⚠️ Illegal Bets (Not Allowed)

  • Big + Small together
  • Betting all answers in same period
  • Big + Red / Big + Green combinations

✅ Allowed Combinations

  • Single color (Red, Green, or Violet)
  • Single size (Big or Small)
  • Single number (1-9)
  • Up to 3 numbers combined with colors/sizes
